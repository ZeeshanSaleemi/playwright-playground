Global Markets Rally After Economic Data Release

Stock markets around the world surged on Friday after the release of better-than-expected employment and inflation data, easing fears of an impending economic slowdown.

Major indices in New York, London, Tokyo, and Frankfurt all posted significant gains, with tech and energy stocks leading the charge.

Economists are cautiously optimistic that the positive trend could continue into the next quarter if consumer spending remains robust.
